Killswitch

Force quit apps instantly from your menu bar.

Privacy Policy

Overview

Killswitch is a lightweight macOS utility that lives in your menu bar and lets you view and force quit running applications. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

Killswitch does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

The app only reads the list of currently running applications on your Mac using standard macOS APIs (NSWorkspace). This information is displayed locally on your screen and is never sent anywhere.

No Analytics

Killswitch contains no analytics, tracking, telemetry, or crash reporting frameworks. No data leaves your device.

No Network Access

Killswitch does not make any network connections. It operates entirely offline.

No Third-Party Services

Killswitch does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Local Storage

The only data Killswitch stores locally is your panel size preference, saved via macOS UserDefaults. No other data is persisted.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the App Store listing.
