## Description
Killswitch lives in your menu bar and does one thing perfectly: lets you force quit any app instantly.

No bloat. No background processes. No CPU usage. Just click the icon, see your running apps, and kill the ones you don't need.

Features:
- One-click force quit from menu bar
- See all running apps at a glance
- Search to find apps quickly
- Adjustable panel size
- Zero background resource usage
- No data collection, fully offline

Killswitch is the Ctrl+Alt+Delete your Mac never had.
