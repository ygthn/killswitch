## Description
Killswitch lives in your menu bar and does one thing perfectly: lets you quit any app instantly.

No bloat. No background processes. No CPU usage. Just click the icon, see your running apps, and quit the ones you don't need.

Features:
- One-click quit from menu bar
- Smart quit with automatic retry for unresponsive apps
- Activity Monitor shortcut when apps won't respond
- See all running apps at a glance
- Search to find apps quickly
- Adjustable panel size
- Zero background resource usage
- No data collection, fully offline

Killswitch is the Ctrl+Alt+Delete your Mac never had.
